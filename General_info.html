Our Mission & Values

We believe reliable data is the cornerstone of justice and good governance. DataWorks turns fragmented legal filings and public datasets into structured, source‑cited intelligence that anyone can verify— then delivers it through tools that serve both commercial legal professionals and public institutions.

  • Transparency First – every insight links back to its source.
  • Rigor & Validation – data pipelines are documented and testable.
  • Public Benefit – our Commons initiative keeps vital civic data open and accessible.

The Breakthrough

At the core of every DataWorks offering is a graph‑based engine that maps people, filings, rulings, statutes, agencies, and outcomes—automatically linking each fact to primary documents. It’s the same infrastructure that powers citation‑grounded AI answers for attorneys and fuels statewide data portals for governments.
