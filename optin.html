Consent to Receive SMS Verification Codes

When a user visits https://thedataworks.org, they are prompted to log in by entering their phone number on the login page.

After submitting their number, the user is explicitly informed that they will receive a 4-digit verification code via SMS. This code is used solely for authentication and expires shortly after it's issued.

By entering their phone number and continuing the login process, the user gives explicit consent to receive a one-time SMS message for authentication purposes.

This login experience, including the phone number entry field and consent messaging, is visible on our login page.

Based on this flow, users provide valid opt-in consent under A2P messaging requirements when initiating login with their phone number.
