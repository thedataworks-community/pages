Modern Legal Intelligence & Public Data Infrastructure

What We Do

DataWorks builds AI-powered legal tools and open civic data infrastructure. We turn fragmented filings, rulings, and public datasets into structured, trustworthy, and actionable intelligence — backed by source citations and public transparency.

Our Product Suite

SummaryCite

Built for law firms, private attorneys, and policy analysts, SummaryCite transforms messy PDFs into structured case timelines and factual summaries. Each fact is citation-backed, making it a fast and trustworthy legal assistant.

LegalCite

Ask legal questions in plain English. LegalCite delivers case law, AG opinions, and statutes — each one sourced, linked, and ready to cite. Smart filters and export-ready output make legal research effortless.

TrialWire

A monthly digest of civil trial outcomes. Attorneys use it to benchmark rulings, monitor damages, and compare courts. It replaces time-consuming case watching with smart, structured updates.

AtlasLaw

Search court filings, track motion outcomes, and uncover case patterns. AtlasLaw combines document chat, citation linking, and judge analytics into one powerful legal intelligence platform.

DataWorks Commons

Open-access public data infrastructure for government and research partners. Distribute and explore row-level data across criminal justice, housing, and behavioral health — all within a user-friendly portal.
